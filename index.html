About Us

Digilogous is the flagship inter-school tech symposium hosted by Rukmini Devi Public School, a premier K-12 institution with over 40 years of academic excellence. Known for its strong foundation in values and innovation, the school seamlessly integrates Artificial Intelligence and modern technology into its curriculum through smart classrooms, advanced labs, and e-learning platforms. Now in its 11th edition, Digilogous brings together the brightest young minds from across Delhi-NCR for a dynamic showcase of coding, creativity, collaboration, and AI-powered thinking through 14+ future-ready events. Join us as future tech leaders converge, ideas ignite, and innovation takes center stage!
